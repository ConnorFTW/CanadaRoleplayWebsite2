About Canada Roleplay

Discover our story and what makes us Canada's premier FiveM roleplay server

Our Community in Action

Welcome to Canada Roleplay

Canada Roleplay was started in 2019 by ConnorFTW and has been growing ever since. We’re a community of over 4,000 members who are all about creating the best roleplay experience. Over the years, our server has gone through a lot of changes, and we’re proud to say we’re the most active Canadian server with some awesome roleplay.

The assets in our server is the result of four years of hard work from actual police officers and fireighters. We have the most high quality Canadian assets you can find on FiveM.

Join Our Community

Our Beginning

Starting as a small group of Canadian FiveM enthusiasts in 2020, we dreamed of creating the most authentic Canadian roleplay experience. Through dedication and community support, we've grown into the server we are today, maintaining our position as Canada's leading FiveM community.

Our Commitment

We're more than just another FiveM server. Our staff team works around the clock to maintain a friendly, drama-free environment where quality roleplay thrives. We're constantly improving and adding new features based on community feedback to ensure the best possible experience.
