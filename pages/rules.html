Please read all rules carefully - not knowing them doesn't exempt you from following them

Guidelines

  • Breaking any of these rules may result in administrative action
  • Use /calladmin to call for staff assistance instead of handling situations yourself
  • All vehicles must be taken from the F10 Menu, not from vMenu
  • You are not allowed to fly airplanes
  • No Sexual Roleplay
  • Keep profanity to a minimum
  • No Hate Speech / Harassment
  • No Suicide Based Roleplay
  • No Discussing Kicks/Bans in Discord -- Tickets Only
  • No Intentionally Starting Drama/Toxicity/Complaining
  • No Server/Community Advertisement
  • No Starting Gangs / Non Passive Groups
  • No using the F5 Menu as a Civilian
  • No Script Abuse / Exploiting
  • Microphone required to play
  • No Trash talk or Negative Talk in chat

Roleplaying

  • Players must do their best to role-play every situation
  • No RDM (Shooting players without reason)
  • No VDM (Hitting players without roleplay)
  • No Unrealistic Driving (Max 175 KMPH)
  • No going out of Character at ANY Time
  • Players must value their lives
  • Pursuits are extremely uncommon in Canada
  • Shootings are less common than in the United States
  • Must roleplay medical injuries correctly
  • No "Cop Baiting"
  • No shooting in green zones
  • No stealing police vehicles
  • No interfering with scenes you're not involved in
  • No kidnapping of any players
  • Keep roleplay groups small and manageable

Priority Cooldown and PeaceTime

  • No fleeing or shooting at police during priority cooldown or peacetime
  • Priorities (which are rare in real life) must be kept to a minimum
  • Running on cooldown will result in immediate kick
  • Alternate between priority and passive roleplay

Passive Roleplay

In roleplay you are supposed to be acting as if you are that person and that is your life. Therefore, all officers and civilians must value their life. Passive roleplay means mixing up priorities (Running from police, shootings, etc) with realistic roleplay (Run a business, do a car show, calls that don't involve violence).
